Welcome to my submission for the MCWT Web Design Competition! Each year the organization chooses a theme for the contestants to design their site around.This year the theme was to "Develop a website for a tech product or service, imaginary or real, that impacts the world in a positive way".I chose to design my website around robotic prosthetics. This site primarily focuses on how these prosthetics work, and how these benefit the people that need them.
