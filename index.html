Introduction

Welcome to my submission to the MCWT Web Design Competition! A little short introduction is needed before you take a tour of the whole website. In regards to the theme, there is not only a difference in time period between myself and my grandmother but also a difference in culture; my grandmother is from Lebanon while I am from the United States so certain aspects may be even more different than if I were to just compare our two time periods. With that out of the way, I hope you enjoy!

Skills and Interests

My likes and skills do differ due to the technological advancements within the last few decades. However, they do partially align because my mother introduced some of these topics to me and due to the fact that I came a family that did not have much either. I love sewing but I use a sewing machine a lot more than my grandmother. I also love video games and doing arts and crafts, like sculpting with polymer clay,digital art and knitting.
